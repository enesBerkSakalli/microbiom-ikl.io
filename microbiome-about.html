About the Nine Microbes Project

The human gut is home to a vast community of microorganisms. In our project, we focus on nine particularly interesting microbes: Roseburia, Bacteroides, Lactobacillus, Prevotella, Faecalibacterium prausnitzii, Bifidobacterium, E. coli, Akkermansia, and Ruminococcus.

By blending art and science, we highlight the distinct forms and potential roles these microbes play in our health. Each illustration is hand-drawn to showcase their unique shapes, from the coiling forms of Roseburia to the spiky bristles of E. coli.

Our Vision

We believe that science communication can be enhanced by an artistic approach, making microscopic worlds accessible and engaging. By blending visuals, storytelling, and interactive elements, we aim to inspire more people to learn about their own internal ecosystems.
